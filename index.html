Priče iz davnine (Croatian tales of long ago)

Priče iz davnine (Croatian tales of long ago) is a short story collection written by the acclaimed children's author Ivana Brlić-Mažuranić, originally published in 1916 in Zagreb by the Matica hrvatska publishing house. The collection is considered her masterpiece.
